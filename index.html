      about Sprinters

      We are passionate and always produce better results.

      We experience the need to feel normal again, to step out without the stress of being exposed to the virus. The times of this ongoing pandemic has made us realize the importance of small things which matter a lot in our lives. One of the significant missing vibes comes from the athletes and runners who are desperately looking forward to getting back to the marathons.
      We bring you the first one of a kind virtual marathon to feel good just like the old days of running marathons without safety concern.

      Run for Good Cause

      This marathon is not just about giving the runners a go at what they love the most. The element of giving towards society is one of the prime directives of Sprinters. With every participant joining the marathon, the contribution towards educating the underprivileged will experience a rise.

      We are trying to do our part and hope you join in to contribute with the same.
